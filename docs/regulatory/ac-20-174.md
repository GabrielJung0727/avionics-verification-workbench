# FAA AC 20-174 — Development of Civil Aircraft and Systems

## Scope (1 line)
FAA Advisory Circular that **accepts SAE ARP4754A** as an acceptable
means (but not the only means) of complying with applicable airworthiness
regulations for civil aircraft and systems development.

## What it says (in plain words)
- ARP4754A is acceptable for system / aircraft-level **development assurance**
- Development assurance levels (IDAL A..E) flow from safety assessment
- Coordinates with ARP4761 for the safety side and DO-178C / DO-254 for
  item-level processes
