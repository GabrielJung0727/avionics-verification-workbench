# FAA AC 20-175 — Controls for Flight Deck Systems

## Scope (1 line)
FAA Advisory Circular providing guidance on **flight-deck controls**
(physical and electronic), how they should be designed and laid out, and
how human-factors objectives apply.

## What it says (in plain words)
- Flight-deck control devices must support the flight-crew's tasks
- Control prioritisation, alerting (warning > caution > advisory),
  colour usage, mode awareness, and confusion mitigation are explicit
  topics
- Aligns with broader human-factors guidance such as AC 25.1322 and
  applicable 14 CFR Part 25 sections
