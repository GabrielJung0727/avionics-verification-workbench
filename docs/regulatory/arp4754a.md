# ARP4754A — Aircraft & System Development Assurance

## Scope (1 line)
Aircraft- and system-level **development assurance** process: requirements
capture, architecture, allocation, change control, validation & verification.

## What it covers
- System development assurance levels (IDAL A..E)
- Top-down requirements decomposition
- Requirements validation (right requirements) vs. verification (built right)
- Architecture & allocation
- Configuration & change management
- Process assurance & coordination with item-level processes (DO-178C, DO-254)

## What is intentionally out of scope
- Real **IDAL allocation** — the levels in this repo are hypotheses
- Coordination with airworthiness authority (AR / DER signoff)
- System safety analysis governance (only the artefacts are mocked)
